Is the ScanSmart Backpack Worth it?

The ScanSmart lay-flat technology allows for laptop-in-case TSA scanning for most portable computers. I have never used this feature, but it’s there.

I purposely bought this Swiss Gear Backpack so I can hold two laptops at the same time and I am very impressed. There have been times I held three laptops in this bad boy! Don’t ask!

The backpack is made out of a combination of fabric and polyester and it holds up great! It has enough cushion that I feel safe setting it on the floor without much impact to my gear.
